## Overview

Your youth center regularly offers special promotions where you can receive digital passes. Promotions appear in the **News** feed and are marked with the **Promotion** badge.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the **News** tab.
2. Scroll through the feed and look out for posts with the **Action** badge.
3. Tap on an action to view the details.
4. You will see: description, image, age range, promotion period and sponsors.
5. The app automatically checks whether you are eligible to take part in the promotion.

## Eligibility check

Not everyone can take part in every promotion. Possible reasons why you are not eligible:

- **"You must be at least X years old "** - You are too young for this promotion.
- **"This promotion is for people up to X years old "** - You are too old for this promotion.
- **"This promotion has ended "** - The promotion period has expired.
- **"This promotion starts on [date]"** - The promotion has not yet started.
- **"You have already used all available redemptions "** - You have reached the limit.
- **"All codes have been used "** - There are no more passes available.

If you already have a pass, you will see the message **"You already have a pass "**.

## FAQ

**Where can I find current promotions?
Promotions appear in the **News** feed between the normal news. Look out for the **Action** badge.

**Can my children also take part in promotions?
Yes, when you receive a pass you can choose whether the pass is for you or one of your children.
