## Overview

Under **My Passes** you will find all your digital passes - both active and already redeemed or expired ones.

## Step-by-step

1. Tap on your profile picture at the top right of the **Current** tab.
2. Select **My Passes** from the user menu.
3. You will see two tabs:
   - **Active** - your currently valid passes
   - **Archive** - redeemed and expired passes

### What the pass cards show

Each card shows you:
- Picture and title of the action
- Who the Pass was created for ("For [name]")
- Validity date ("Valid until [date]")
- Status badge
- Pass code

### Pass status

- **Active** - the pass is valid and can be used
- **Redeemed** - the pass has been redeemed (with date and time: "Redeemed on [date]")
- **Expired** - the validity period has expired

### Show QR code

Tap on a pass card to display the QR code. You can show this if required.

## FAQ

**Can I also display my Pass without the Internet?
Yes, saved passes can also be displayed offline. You will then see the message "Saved pass is displayed (offline)".

**What happens if my passport has expired?
Expired passes are moved to the **Archive** tab and can no longer be used.

**Where can I find the QR code for my Pass?
Tap on the Pass card in **My Passes** to display the QR code in detail.
