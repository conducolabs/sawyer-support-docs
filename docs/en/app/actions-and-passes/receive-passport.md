## Overview

If you are eligible for a promotion, you can get a digital pass. The pass contains a QR code and can be saved to your wallet or downloaded as a PDF.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the details page of a promotion in the **News** feed.
2. Tap on **Receive pass**.
3. **Who is this pass for** - Select:
   - **For me** - the pass is for you
   - One of your children - if you have created child profiles
4. You can also create passes for multiple children via **Log in your children**.
5. Tap on **Confirm**.
6. Your pass will be created. You will see the message "Your passport is being created..."
7. After creation, the Pass with QR code will be displayed and you will receive the confirmation **Pass created!
8. A copy will automatically be sent to your e-mail address.
9. You now have the following options:
   - **Add to Apple Wallet** (iPhone)
   - **Add to Google Wallet** (Android)
   - **Download PDF**
   - **Share**
10. Tap **Done** or **View my passes** to see all your passes.

## FAQ

**Can I create a Pass for several children at the same time?
Yes, you can create passes for your children using the **Register your children** option.

**Do I need to be logged in?
Yes, you must have an account and be logged in to get a Pass.
