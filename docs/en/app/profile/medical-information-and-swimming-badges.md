## Overview

You can enter medical information and the status of your swimming badge in your profile. This information can be passed on to the leaders of your activities so that they are informed in an emergency.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Edit profile** via your user menu.
2. Scroll to the **Allergies / Medical information** section.
3. Enter relevant information, e.g. allergies, medication or other medical information.
4. In the **Swimming badge** section, you can activate the **Has a swimming badge** button if you have a swimming badge.
5. Tap on **Save changes**.

## FAQ

**Who can see my medical information?
The information may be shared with the leaders of the activities you participate in. They are used for safety at events.

**Is the information mandatory?
The medical information is optional but recommended for participants under the age of 21. The swimming badge may be relevant for certain water-based activities.
