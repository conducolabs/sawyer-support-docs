## Overview

Your profile picture is displayed in various places in the app, e.g. in the user menu and when you log in. You can upload a photo or change your existing picture at any time.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Edit profile** via your user menu.
2. Tap on **Change photo** under your current profile picture.
3. Select a photo from your gallery.
4. Crop the image if required.
5. The photo will be uploaded automatically. You will see the message "Photo being uploaded...".

As long as you have not uploaded a profile picture, your initials will be displayed instead.

## Troubleshooting

**"Photo could not be uploaded "**
Make sure you have an active internet connection and try again. Also check if the app has access to your photo gallery.
