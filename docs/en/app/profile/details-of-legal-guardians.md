## Overview

If you are under 21 years of age, you must enter the contact details of a parent or guardian in your profile. This information is mandatory and is required to register for activities.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Edit profile** via your user menu.
2. Scroll to the **Parent:r** section.
3. Enter the contact details:
   - **First name** and **Last name** of the legal guardian
   - **Phone number**
4. Tap on **Save changes**.

## FAQ

**Why do I need to enter this information?
Your youth center needs a contact person for emergencies and organizational questions regarding activities.

**I am over 21 - do I still have to provide this information?
No. The **Guardian:r** section is only displayed if you are under 21 years old. The note reads: "Mandatory information for participants under the age of 21".

**What happens if I don't fill in the fields?
You may not be able to register for activities as the fields are mandatory.
