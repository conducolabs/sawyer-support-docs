## Overview

You can manage your personal details and address in your profile. Keep your details up to date so that registration for activities runs smoothly.

## Step-by-step

1. Tap on your profile picture at the top right of the **Current** tab.
2. Select **Edit profile** from the user menu. Alternatively, you can also find the option in the **Menu** tab.
3. Edit the desired fields:
   - **Personal data**: **First name**, **Last name**, **Email address**, **Phone number**, **Birthday**
   - **Address**: **Street**, **Postcode**, **City**
4. Tap on **Save changes**.

Fields marked with an asterisk or the note **mandatory field** must be completed.

## FAQ

**What happens if I change my e-mail address?
You have to confirm your new e-mail address again. You will receive a confirmation email to the new address.

**Can I change my birthday later?
Yes, you can change your birthday in your profile. Please note that your age may be relevant for participation in certain activities.

## Troubleshooting

**"Please fill in all mandatory fields "**
Information is still missing in one or more mandatory fields. Check whether all marked fields are filled in.

**"Profile could not be updated "**
Try again and make sure you have an active internet connection.
