## Overview

You can register for activities directly in the app. You select who is taking part, accept the conditions of participation and choose a payment method.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the details page of the activity and tap on **Sign up now**.

2. **Who is taking part** - Select who should take part in the activity:
   - **Yourself** - yourself
   - Your children - if you have created children's profiles, you can select them here
   - You can register several people at the same time
   - If someone is too young or too old, a message will be displayed
   - The price is displayed as **Total: [price]** (for multiple participants **per participant:in**)

3. Tap on **Next**.

4. **Conditions of participation** - Read the conditions and activate **I accept the conditions of participation**.

5. Tap on **Next**.

6. **Select payment method** - Select one of the available payment methods (e.g. Apple Pay, credit card, PayPal, cash). This step is not required for free activities.

7. Tap on **Pay now** (or **Confirm** for cash payment/free activities).

8. If you are paying online, you will be redirected to a secure payment page. Complete the payment there.

9. After completion you will see your status:
   - **Confirmed - all done** - registration and payment successful
   - **Pending - please pay on site ** - Cash payment selected
   - **Pending - awaiting confirmation - registration must be confirmed by the organizer

10. Tap on **Done**.

## FAQ

**Can I register myself and my children at the same time?
Yes, in the first step you can select multiple participants.

**What happens if there are only a few places left?
You will see the message "Only X places available". If there are not enough places available for all selected participants, you will be informed.

## Troubleshooting

**"There are not enough places available for this activity. "**
Reduce the number of participants or try again later.

**"Please log in to participate "**
You must be logged in to register for an activity.
