## Overview

Payment for activities is made directly in the app. Different payment methods are available depending on the activity. Free activities do not require payment.

## Available payment methods

The payment methods available depend on the activity in question. Possible options are:

- **Apple Pay** - payment via Apple Pay on your iPhone
- **credit card** - payment by credit card via a secure payment page
- **giropay** - direct transfer via your bank account
- **PayPal** - payment via your PayPal account
- **Sofort / Klarna** - instant bank transfer via your bank account
- **MünsterlandCard** - payment with the regional MünsterlandCard
- **Cash (pay on site)** - Cash payment on site at the event

## How the payment works

For online payment methods (everything except cash payment), you will be redirected to a secure payment page after making your selection. Complete the payment there. You will then automatically return to the app.

### Status after payment

- **"Payment confirmed - all done!"** - Your payment was successful.
- **"Payment being processed... "** - The payment is still being processed.
- **"Pending - please pay on site. "** - You have selected cash payment and are paying on site.
- **"Payment has not been completed. Your registration is pending. "** - The payment has been canceled. You can register again as long as there are still places available.

## FAQ

**Which payment methods are accepted for my activity?
The available payment methods are displayed as icons on the activity details page and appear for selection during the registration process.

**What happens if I cancel the payment?
Your registration will remain as "pending". You can register again and complete the payment as long as there are still places available.

**Is the payment secure?
Yes, all online payments are processed through a secure payment provider. The app does not store any bank or credit card details.
