## Overview

If an activity is fully booked, you can be placed on the waiting list. As soon as a place becomes available, you will be informed by push notification and can register.

## Step-by-step

### Add to the waiting list

1. Open the details page of a fully booked activity.
2. Tap on **Add to waiting list**.
3. You will receive the confirmation: "We will notify you when places become available for this event."

### When a place becomes available

You will receive a push notification with the message **Spaces available** and the message: "The activity you were waiting for now has free spaces. Sign up now before they're gone!"

Open the activity and sign up as normal.

### Leave waiting list

1. Open the details page of the activity for which you are on the waiting list.
2. Tap on **Leave waiting list**.
3. Confirm with **Leave waiting list**.

After leaving the waiting list, you will no longer receive notifications for this activity.

## FAQ

**Do I automatically get a place if someone cancels?
No, you will only be notified that places have become available. You will then have to register yourself. The principle "First come. First serve."

**Do I need to have push notifications activated?
For waiting list notifications, you should have push notifications activated so that you are informed immediately when places become available.
