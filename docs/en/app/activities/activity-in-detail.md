## Overview

If you tap on an activity, the details page opens with all the important information about the event.

## What you will find on the detail page

- **Image and title** of the activity
- **Description** with detailed information
- **Costs** - the participation price or the note **Free of charge**
- **Dates** - all dates and times of the event
- **Leaders** - the supervisors of the activity with profile pictures
- **Organizer** - the club or organization with logo, telephone number, email and website
- **Venue** - the address with map view. Tap on **Open in maps** to open the location in your map app.
- **Pick-up location** - if a separate pick-up location is specified

### Age restrictions

Some activities have age restrictions. These are displayed on the details page, for example
- "This offer is aimed at participants aged X-Y years."
- "This offer is aimed at participants aged X and over."

### Available payment methods

The accepted payment methods are displayed as symbols at the bottom of the details page (e.g. Apple Pay, credit card, PayPal and others).

### Share activity

Tap **Share** to share the link to the activity with others.

## FAQ

**What does "Unfortunately fully booked" mean?
All places are full. In this case, you can be put on the waiting list.

**What does "Already registered" mean?
You are already registered for this activity.

**What does "Registration possible from [date]" mean?
Registration is not yet open and will start on the date shown.
