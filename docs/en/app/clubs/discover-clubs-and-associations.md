## Overview

In the app you can discover clubs and associations from your region. You will find information about the organizations, their contact details and the activities they offer.

## Step-by-step

1. Tap on the **Menu** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. Select **Clubs & Associations** from the menu.
3. You will see a list of all clubs with logo, name and description.
4. Tap on a club to open the details page.

### What you will find on the detail page

- **ABOUT US** - Description of the club
- **YOUR CONTACT TO US** - contact details such as telephone number, e-mail address and website
- **ACTIVITIES** - all events organized by this association

Tap on an activity to go directly to the details page and registration.

## FAQ

**A club shows no activities - why?
If "No activities available" is displayed, the club currently has no upcoming events.

**Can I join a club directly via the app?
The app shows you the club's contact details. To become a member, contact the club directly using the contact details provided.
