## Overview

In the **Apprenticeships** tab, you can search for specific job vacancies, companies or occupational fields to quickly find the right one for you.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the **Training** tab.
2. Tap on the search symbol (**Search**).
3. Enter at least 4 characters in the search field. The placeholder text is: "Search for jobs, companies or professions..."
4. The results are displayed automatically and are grouped into categories:
   - **Companies** - matching companies with logo
   - **Job offers** - specific vacancies
   - **Professions** - suitable occupational fields
5. Tap on a result to display the details.

## FAQ

**Why are no results displayed?
Make sure that you have entered at least 4 characters. If nothing is found for your search term, the following message will appear: "Sorry, we could not find anything for the search term '[term]'. Please try again." Try other search terms or more general terms.

**Can I also search for company names?
Yes, the search searches both professions and job vacancies as well as company names.
