## Overview

When you tap on a company or position, you will see all the details about the apprenticeship or internship. Here you will find everything you need for your application.

## What you will find on the details page

- **About us** - information about the company with logo and website
- **What makes the apprenticeship special** - what makes the apprenticeship at this company special
- **About the apprenticeship** / **About the internship** - general description of the professional field
- **Your tasks** - what you can expect during the apprenticeship
- **Your profile** - what requirements you should bring with you
- **Training duration** - duration of the training in years
- **Vocational school** - name and location of the associated vocational school
- **Training location** - where the training takes place
- **Certificate** - minimum school-leaving qualification required
- **Your contact to us** - contact person in the company with contact details

## Apply or request an internship

- Tap on **Apply now** to apply for an apprenticeship.
- Tap on **Inquire internship** to submit an internship request.

The application is made directly via the contact person provided.

## Share job offer

Tap **Share** to share the job offer with friends or family.

## FAQ

**How do I apply?
You will be put in contact with the company via the **Apply now** or **Request internship** button. The exact application steps depend on the company in question.

**Where does the job information come from?
General career information is marked "From the Berufenet" and comes from the Federal Employment Agency.
