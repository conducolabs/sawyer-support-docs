## Overview

In the **Apprenticeships** tab, you will find apprenticeships and internship opportunities from companies in your region. The offers are sorted by occupational field so that you can quickly discover suitable positions.

## Step-by-step

1. Tap on the **Apprenticeships** tab in the bottom navigation bar.
2. You will see a list of occupational fields with pictures and descriptions.
3. Each card shows you how many jobs and companies are available (e.g. "3 vacancies at 2 companies").
4. Tap on an occupational field to display the details and companies.
5. You can switch between two tabs in the detailed view:
   - **Apprenticeship** - Apprenticeships
   - **Internship** - Internship opportunities
6. You will see the companies offering jobs in this occupational field with logos and available positions.

If **Internships are possible** is displayed, companies in this occupational field also offer internships.

## FAQ

**What is the difference between an apprenticeship and an internship?
An **apprenticeship** is several years of vocational training leading to a qualification. An **internship** is a shorter insight into a profession to gain experience.

**Why can't I see any vacancies?
If "No vacancies available" is displayed, there are currently no vacancies. Check back regularly as new vacancies may be added.
