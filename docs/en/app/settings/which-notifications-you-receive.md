## Overview

You can individually set which types of notifications you would like to receive. This way, you only receive the information that is relevant to you.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the **Menu** tab.
2. Tap on **Notification settings**.
3. Activate or deactivate the individual notification types according to your wishes.

## Notification types

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Subscription status** | Notifications when your subscription is confirmed or rejected |
**Payment status** | Payment confirmations and receipts |
| **Activity changes** | When activity details or times change |
**Activity reminders** | Reminders before upcoming activities |
| Announcements** | Important updates and news |

## FAQ

**Can I deactivate individual notifications without turning them all off?
Yes, you can switch each notification type on and off individually.

**Why am I not receiving any notifications despite having activated the settings?
Make sure that push notifications are always activated (both in the app and in the device settings). You must also be logged in.
