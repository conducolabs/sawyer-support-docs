## Overview

In the **Menu** tab, you will find additional content for your youth center, settings and legal information. The menu items may vary depending on the facility.

## What you will find in the menu

### Contents of your youth center

Depending on the facility, different pages are displayed in the menu - for example information pages, contact details or links to external offers. This content is maintained by your youth center and may change.

### Legal information

At the bottom of the menu you will find the legal pages:
- **Imprint** - legal information about the operator of the app
- **Privacy Policy** - information on the handling of your data
- **Terms of use** - the terms of use of the app

### App version and updates

The current version of the app is displayed at the bottom. If a new version is available, a banner will appear: **"A new version is available. Update now!"** Tap on it to go to the App Store or Google Play Store.

### Appearance

The app automatically follows the display setting of your device (light or dark mode). You will see the message "Automatically uses dark mode (system setting)" or "Automatically uses light mode (system setting)".

## FAQ

**Why do I see different menu items than other users?
The menu items are managed by your youth center and may differ between different facilities.

**How can I switch between dark mode and light mode?
The app automatically adopts the display setting of your device. Change the setting in the system settings of your smartphone.
