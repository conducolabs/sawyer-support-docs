## Overview

Push notifications keep you up to date - whether it's waiting list updates, news about your activities or important app updates. You can switch the notifications on or off at any time.

## Step-by-step

### Activate notifications on first start

The first time you open the app or the notification settings, an introduction appears with the title **Stay informed** and the benefits:
- Receive waiting list updates
- Get news about your activities
- Don't miss important app updates

Tap **Enable notifications** to allow push notifications. Or select **Decide later** to activate them later.

### Activate notifications later

1. Open the **Menu** tab.
2. Tap on **Notification settings**.
3. Tap on **Enable notifications**.

### Authorization has been denied

If you have denied permission at device level, the following message will appear: "To receive notifications, please enable them in your device settings." Open the system settings of your device and allow notifications for the app.

## FAQ

**Do I need to be logged in?
Yes, you need to be logged in to manage your notification settings.

**I'm not getting any notifications - what can I do?
Check in the device settings whether notifications are allowed for the app. You should see the status **Push notifications are enabled** in the app.
