## Overview

Some devices give you access to smart functions via the app - for example, door openers or other controls. These functions are tied to certain times and access rights.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the setup page via the **menu** or a shared link.
2. You will see an overview of the setup with a picture, description and available functions.
3. Tap on a function to trigger it.
4. After successful execution, the confirmation "Done" appears.

### Schedule

Each function has a schedule that shows you when it is available:
- Availability is listed by day of the week (**Monday** to **Sunday**) with the respective times.
- **"Available until [time]"** - the function is currently available.
- **"Opens at [time]"** - the function is currently not available and will be activated at the specified time.

### Requirements

- You must be logged in to access facilities.
- Depending on the facility, you may need certain access rights.

## Troubleshooting

**"Please log in to access this facility "**
Log in with your account to use the facility.

**"You do not have access to this facility "**
You do not have the necessary access rights. Contact your youth center.

**"This function is currently not available "**
The function is out of schedule. Check the schedule for availability.

**"Connection failed" / "The request took too long "**
Check your internet connection and try **Try again**.
