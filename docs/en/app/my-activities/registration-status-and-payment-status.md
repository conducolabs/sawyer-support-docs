## Overview

After registering for an activity, your registration can have different statuses. Here you can find out what they mean and what you need to do.

## Status overview

### Confirmed

**Confirmed - all done!

Your registration is confirmed and payment has been received (or the activity is free of charge). You don't need to do anything else.

### Payment pending

**Payment pending**

The payment has not yet been completed. There may be various reasons for this:
- You have canceled or not completed the online payment.
- The payment is still being processed.

You can register for the activity again and complete the payment as long as there are still places available.

### Pending - cash payment

**Pending - please pay on site **

You have selected **Cash (pay on site)** as payment method. Pay the amount on site on the day of the event.

### Pending - confirmation

**Pending - waiting for confirmation **

Your registration must be confirmed manually by the organizer. You will be notified as soon as your registration is confirmed or rejected.

## FAQ

**My payment has been canceled - what can I do?
You can re-register for the activity and complete the payment as long as there are still places available.

**How long does it take for my registration to be confirmed?
This depends on the organizer. Activate push notifications to be informed about status changes.
