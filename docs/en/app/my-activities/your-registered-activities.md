## Overview

Under **My registrations** you will find all the activities for which you or your children are registered. You can switch between upcoming and past activities.

## Step-by-step

1. Tap on your profile picture at the top right of the **Current** tab.
2. Select **My logins** from the user menu.
3. You will see two tabs:
   - **Upcoming** - upcoming activities
   - **Archive** - past activities

### What the cards show

Each activity card shows you:
- Picture and title of the activity
- Date and time (for multiple dates: **other dates**)
- Number of people registered (e.g. "2 people registered")
- If a payment is outstanding: **Payment outstanding**

### View registered participants

Tap on the number of participants to see all registered persons. Each person is displayed with name and status badge:
- **You** - yourself
- **Confirmed** - registration confirmed
- **Payment pending** - payment still outstanding

### Navigate to the activity

Tap on a card to open the detail page of the activity.

## FAQ

**Where can I find past activities?
Go to the **Archive** tab to see all the activities you have already taken part in.

**Why is the list empty?
If "No upcoming activities" is displayed, you are not currently registered for any activity. Tap on **Browse activities** to discover new offers.
