## Overview

If your child is old enough, you can convert their child profile into an independent user account. Your child can then log in to the app with their own e-mail address and password.

**Important:** This process cannot be undone.

## Step-by-step

1. Open **Manage children** via your user menu.
2. Tap on the child profile you want to convert.
3. Tap on **Create a standalone account**.
4. Read the note: "This will convert [name] into an independent user account. The child can then log in with their own email and password. This action cannot be undone."
5. Tap on **Continue**.
6. Enter a **new email address** for the child.
7. Set a **password** (at least 8 characters) and confirm it.
8. Tap on **Create account**.

After the conversion, the confirmation "[Name] now has an independent account" appears. The child will be removed from your child list and can now log in independently.

## Troubleshooting

**"This e-mail address is already in use "**
Use a different e-mail address that is not yet linked to an account.

**"Passwords do not match "**
Make sure that you enter exactly the same password in both password fields.

**"Password must be at least 8 characters long "**
Choose a password with at least 8 characters.
