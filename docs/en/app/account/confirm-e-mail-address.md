## Overview

After registering or changing your e-mail address, you must confirm it in order to use all the app functions. Some functions are not available to you without a confirmed e-mail address.

## Step-by-step

### Confirm e-mail

1. Check your e-mail inbox after registration.
2. Open the confirmation email and click on the link.
3. Go back to the app and tap **I have confirmed my email** to update your status.

### Send confirmation email again

If you have not received an email, tap **Resend confirmation email** in the app. Also check your spam folder.

### Change e-mail address

1. Tap on **Change email address** on the confirmation screen.
2. Enter your new e-mail address.
3. Tap on **Change email**.
4. You will receive a new confirmation email to the changed address.

## FAQ

**What happens if I change my e-mail address?
A new confirmation e-mail will be sent to your new address. Until the new address is confirmed, some functions may be restricted.

## Troubleshooting

**"Your email has not yet been confirmed "**
Click on the link in the confirmation email and then tap on **I have confirmed my email** in the app.

**"This email address is already in use. "**
The new email address is already linked to another account. Use a different email address.
