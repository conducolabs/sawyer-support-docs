## Overview

You can permanently delete your account at any time. All your data will be irrevocably removed. This process cannot be reversed.

## Step-by-step

1. Open your user menu (profile picture in the top right of the **Current** tab).
2. Tap on **Edit profile**.
3. Scroll all the way down.
4. Tap on **Delete account**.
5. Confirm with **Delete irrevocably**.

## FAQ

**What happens to my data?
All your personal data, registrations and passports will be permanently deleted. You will receive the confirmation "Account deleted".

**Can I restore my account after deletion?
No. The deletion is final and cannot be reversed. However, you can re-register with a new e-mail address at any time.

**I just want to take a break - do I have to delete my account?
No, you can simply log out and use the app again later. Your account will remain active.
