## Overview

If you have forgotten your password, you can reset it via the app. You will receive an email with instructions on how to create a new password.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the login screen.
2. Tap on **Forgot your password? Reset now**.
3. Enter your e-mail address.
4. Tap on **Send reset link**.
5. Check your email inbox (including the spam folder) and follow the instructions in the email to set a new password.

## FAQ

**I have not received an e-mail - what can I do?
Check your spam folder. If you still haven't received an e-mail after a few minutes, make sure you have entered the correct e-mail address. An e-mail will only be sent if an account with this address exists.

## Troubleshooting

**"Reset failed. Please try again. "**
Try again in a few minutes. Make sure you have an active internet connection.
