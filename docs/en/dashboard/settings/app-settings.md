# App settings

Under **Settings > App settings** you configure the basic settings of the app as a super admin.

## General settings

- **Imprint URL** (mandatory field) - The URL to your imprint. Must be a valid HTTPS address.
- **Privacy URL** (mandatory field) - The URL to your privacy policy. Must be a valid HTTPS address.
- **General terms and conditions** (mandatory field) - The terms and conditions of your app. Edited via a rich text editor with full formatting.

Click **Save** to apply the changes.

## AI Credits default values

Here you define the standard credit quotas:

- **Standard credits for clubs** - How many monthly AI credits a new club receives by default.
- **Standard credits for companies** - How many monthly AI credits a new company receives by default.

Click **Save** to update the values.

## Menu management

Here you can manage the menu items that are displayed in the app.

### Add menu item

Click on **Add menu item** and enter the following information:

- **Name** - The displayed text of the menu item.
- **URL/Link** - The target URL of the menu item.
- **Icon** (optional) - An icon for the menu item.

### Change order

The menu items can be put in the desired order using **drag-and-drop**. The sorting is saved automatically.

### Edit menu item

Click on **Edit** to change the data of a menu item.

### Delete menu item

Click on **Delete** to remove a menu item.
