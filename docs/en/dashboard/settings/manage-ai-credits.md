# Manage AI credits

Under **Settings > AI Credits** you can manage the AI credits required for the use of AI functions (e.g. text generation, image generation) as a super admin.

## Account balance

Four cards show you the current balance:

- **Monthly credits** - The credits available this month.
- **Bonus credits** - Credits allocated once.
- **Credits used** - Credits already used.
- **Credits remaining** - Credits still available.

## Assign credits

In this section you assign credits to clubs or companies.

1. **Target type** - Choose whether you want to assign credits to a club or a company.
2. **Target** - Select the specific club or company from the dropdown.
3. Select the type of allocation:
   - **Monthly** - Sets a monthly recurring credit quota.
   - **One-time** - Assigns one-time bonus credits (minimum 1).
4. Enter the number of credits and click **Assign**.

## Usage history

The table shows the usage of AI Credits:

- **Action** - Which AI function was used (e.g. text generation).
- **Consumption** - How many credits were used.
- **Status** - Whether the generation was successful, failed or is still running (color-coded badge).
- **User:in** - Who has used the function.
- **Club** - Which club the use is assigned to.

### Filter

You can use the dropdown above to filter the usage history by a specific club or display all entries for the entire account.

25 entries per page are displayed.
