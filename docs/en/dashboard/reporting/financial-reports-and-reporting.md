# Financial reports and reporting

Under **Reporting** in the club navigation you will find detailed financial reports and statistics on the activities of your club.

## Select period

Select the desired reporting period:

- **Start date** - By default, 30 days before today's date.
- **End date** - Today's date by default.

**Note:** The maximum period is 3 months.

## Summary

Four statistics cards show you the most important key figures:

- **Total turnover** - The total turnover in the selected period in euros.
- **Available funds** - Currently available funds in euros.
- **Future funds** - Amounts still outstanding in euros.
- **Refunds** - Total amount and number of refunds.

## Time history diagram

An area chart shows the progress over the selected period:

- **X-axis** - Date
- **Left Y-axis** - Turnover in euros
- **Right Y-axis** - number of participants

Move the mouse pointer over the diagram to view details on individual days.

## Payment methods

A horizontal bar chart shows the turnover broken down by payment method:

- PayPal
- Credit card
- Apple Pay
- MünsterlandCard
- Free of charge

Below, each payment method is listed with the respective turnover and number of transactions.

## Activity overview

A table (on mobile device cards) shows for each event in the period:

- **activity name**
- **Date** and time
- **Ticket price** in Euro
- **Registrations** - number of confirmed / total registrations
- **Turnover** in Euro
- **Refunds** in Euro

## CSV export

Click on the **Export CSV** button to download the data as a CSV file. The button is deactivated during the export.
