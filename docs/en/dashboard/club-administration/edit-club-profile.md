# Edit club profile

You can edit your club's profile under **Profile** in the club navigation.

## Fields in the club profile

- **Club name** (mandatory field) - The name of your club.
- **Description** (mandatory field) - A description of your club. Here you can also use the AI support of **conduco AI** to refine the text or have it generated.
- **Website** (mandatory field) - The web address of your club. Must start with `https://`.
- **E-mail** (mandatory field) - The contact e-mail address of the association.
- **Phone** (mandatory) - The club's telephone number in international format (e.g. +49 ...).
- **Logo** (optional) - The club logo. Can be uploaded via the image upload.
- **Cover picture** (optional) - A cover picture for the club. You can also have an image generated by AI here.

## AI support

Next to the description field and the cover picture you will find AI buttons:

- **Description**: Click on the AI button to have a description generated or to improve an existing one.
- **Cover picture**: Click on the AI button to generate a cover image based on the club name and description.

AI credits are required to use the AI functions.

## Save

The **Save** button is only active once you have made changes and all mandatory fields have been filled in correctly. Incorrect fields are highlighted in red.
