# Manage facilities

Under **Facilities & Places > Facilities** you manage the facilities in the system as a super admin.

## Facilities list

The list shows each facility:

- **Name** of the facility
- **Address** (street, zip code, city)
- **GPS coordinates** - display whether coordinates are stored

25 facilities are displayed per page. The list appears as a map on mobile devices.

## Create facility

Click on **Add facility**. You will be redirected to the form page.

### Form fields

- **Name** (mandatory field) - The name of the facility.
- **Slug** (mandatory field) - Is automatically generated from the name (lower case letters, hyphens).
- **Description** (optional) - A description of the facility (max. 2000 characters).
- **Image** (optional) - An image of the facility.
- **Access level** (mandatory field) - Choose between:
  - Public
  - Authenticated (only logged in users)
  - Restricted
- **Location** - Address with interactive map selection:
  - Address search with geocoding
  - Interactive map (300px height)
  - Latitude (-90 to 90) and longitude (-180 to 180)

## Edit facility

Click on **Edit** for a facility to go to the form page.

## Show facility details

Click on **View details** to see the complete information of a facility in a detailed view.

## Delete facility

Click on **Delete** and confirm the action in the confirmation dialog.
