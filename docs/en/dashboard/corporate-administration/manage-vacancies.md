# Manage vacancies

You can manage your company's jobs under **Job vacancies** in the company navigation.

## Create job offer

Click on **Add job** and fill out the form:

- **Job type** (mandatory field) - Select one of the predefined job types. This field is only available when creating.
- **Description** (mandatory field) - A description of the job offer.
- **Special features** (mandatory field) - The most important features of the job.
- **Type of training** (mandatory field) - How the training is organized:
  - Exclusively in the company
  - Both in the training workshop/department and in the company
  - Exclusively in the training workshop/department
- **Duration of training** (mandatory field) - Duration in years (1, 2, 2.5, 3, 3.5 or 4 years).
- **Vocational school** (mandatory field) - Name of the associated vocational school.
- **Minimum qualification** (mandatory field) - The required school-leaving qualification:
  - No qualification
  - Secondary school leaving certificate
  - Intermediate school leaving certificate
  - Fachabitur
  - A-levels
- **Tasks** (optional) - A list of tasks that can be sorted using drag-and-drop. Individual entries can be added and removed.
- **Requirements** (optional) - A list of requirements, which can also be sorted.
- **Contact person:in** (mandatory field) - Select one of the created contact persons.
- **Number of available positions** (mandatory field) - How many positions are advertised.
- **Internships available** - Toggle whether internships are offered.
- **Visible** - Toggle whether the job offer is displayed in the app.

## Edit job offer

Click on **Edit** in the Actions column to edit an existing job offer. The job type cannot be changed afterwards.

## Advertisements

You can also manage **advertisements** for a job offer via the Actions menu.

## Delete job offer

Click on **Delete** in the Actions menu and confirm the action.

## Overview

In the list you can see each job offer:

- Job type symbol
- Job title
- Visibility status (eye symbol)
- Number of available jobs
- Name of contact person
