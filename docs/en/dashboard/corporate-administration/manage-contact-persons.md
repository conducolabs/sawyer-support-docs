# Manage contact persons

Under **Contact persons** in the company navigation, you can manage your company's contact persons. These are used, for example, as contact persons for job vacancies.

## Add contact person

Click on **Add contact** and fill in the following fields:

- **First name** (mandatory field)
- **Last name** (mandatory field)
- **Job title** (mandatory field) - The person's position in the company.
- **E-mail** (mandatory field) - A valid e-mail address.
- **Phone** (mandatory field) - The contact person's telephone number.
- **Profile picture** (optional) - A photo of the contact person.

## Edit contact person

Click on **Edit** for the desired person to change the data in the form.

## Delete contact person

You can also delete a contact person via the edit function. The action must be confirmed in the confirmation dialog.

## Overview

All contact persons are displayed in the list with avatar, name, job title, e-mail and telephone number.
