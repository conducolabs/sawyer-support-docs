# Manage company settings

You can find your company settings under **Settings** in the company navigation.

## Visibility

Use the **Visibility toggle** to control whether your company is displayed in the app.

## Admin management

This is where you manage who has administrative access to your company.

### Add admin

1. Enter the e-mail address of the person you want to add as an admin.
2. Click on the Add button or press Enter.

### Remove admin

1. Click on the Remove button next to the respective admin.
2. Confirm the action in the confirmation dialog.

**Note:** You cannot remove yourself as an admin.

### Admin list

In the list you can see all current admins with avatar, name and e-mail address.
