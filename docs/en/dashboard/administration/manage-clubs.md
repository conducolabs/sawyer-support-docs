# Manage clubs

Under **Administration > Clubs** you will find an overview of all clubs as a super admin.

## Club list

The list shows per club:

- **Logo** of the club
- **Club name**
- **Active/Inactive toggle** - Switch a club on or off

On mobile devices the clubs are displayed as cards, on desktop devices as a table. 25 clubs are displayed per page, incl. total number and page navigation.

## Add club

Click on **Add club** to create a new club. The dialog will guide you through the necessary information.

## Activate / deactivate club

You can quickly activate or deactivate individual clubs using the toggle in the club list. Deactivated clubs are not visible in the app.
