# Manage company

Under **Administration > Companies** you will find an overview of all companies as a super admin.

## Company list

The list shows per company:

- **Logo** of the company
- **Company name**
- **Active/Inactive toggle** - Switch a company on or off

On mobile devices the companies are displayed as cards, on desktop devices as a table. 25 entries are displayed per page.

## Add company

Click on **Add company** to create a new company. The dialog will guide you through the necessary information.

## Activate / deactivate company

You can quickly activate or deactivate individual companies using the toggle in the list. Deactivated companies are not visible in the app.
