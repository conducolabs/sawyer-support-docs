# Manage participants

You can view and manage the participants for each activity. To do this, click on **Participants** for the desired activity.

## List of participants

In the overview you can see all registered participants with:

- Name
- Status (Confirmed / Pending)
- Payment status (Paid / Pending / Free)

Click on a participant to open the detailed view.

## Add participant

1. Click on **Add participant:in**.
2. **Step 1**: Enter the person's e-mail address and click on Search.
3. **Step 2**: Select the desired people from the results list. Child accounts are also displayed.
   - People who are already registered are highlighted.
   - Age indications show you whether the person is within the age limit for the event.
4. Click on **Add** to complete the registration.

## Participant:in-Details

In the detail view you will find:

- **Personal data**: Name, e-mail, telephone, date of birth and age.
- **Parent information**: Name and phone number of parents (if available).
- **Allergies / Medical Information**: Registered medical information.
- **Swimming badge**: Indication of whether the person can swim.
- **Payment information**: Payment status and payment method (e.g. PayPal, credit card, Apple Pay, cash payment, MünsterlandCard, free of charge).

### Confirm payment

For cash payments or MünsterlandCard payments with the status "Created", you can confirm the payment manually. To do this, click on **Confirm payment** and confirm the action in the dialog.

### Remove participant:in

Click on **Remove** and confirm the action in the confirmation dialog to remove a participant from the activity. This will also perform a refund of online payments.

## Export participant list

Click on the **Download** button to download the participant list as a CSV file. Before exporting, you must confirm the terms of use via checkbox.
