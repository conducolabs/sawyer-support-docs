# Manage advertising material

You can create and manage advertising material for activities, vacancies and home page content.

## Open advertising material editor

Click on the **Advertising material** option for the desired activity, training position or start page entry.

## Edit fields

Different fields are available depending on the content type:

### For activities
- **Title** (optional) - The promotional title of the event.
- **Subtitle** (optional) - An additional subtitle.

### For job offers
- **Job title** (optional) - The advertising title of the job.
- **Company name** (optional) - The name of the company.

### For home page content
- **Headline** (optional) - The advertising headline.
- **Subline** (optional) - A supplementary line.

## Language

You can use the language selector to create the ad creatives in different languages (German and English).
