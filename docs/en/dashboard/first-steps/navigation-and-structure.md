# Navigation and structure

The dashboard can be navigated via a sidebar. On mobile devices, you can access the navigation via the hamburger menu at the top left.

## Sidebar

The sidebar is divided into several expandable areas:

### Global navigation (Super Admin)

- **Dashboard** - The start page with statistics and overview.
- **Administration** - Manage users, clubs and companies.
- **Content** - Maintain start page, categories and job types.
- **Facilities & locations** - Manage facilities and MünsterlandCard if applicable.
- **Communication** - Notifications and promotions (if activated).
- **Settings** - App settings, AI credits and activity log.

### Club navigation

If you have selected a club, the following will also appear:

- **Activities** - Manage club events.
- **Reporting** - Financial reports and statistics.
- **Locations** - Manage event locations.
- **Profile** - Edit club profile.
- **Settings** - Club settings and admins.

### Company navigation

If you have selected a company, the following will also appear:

- **Job offers** - Manage the company's jobs.
- **Contact persons** - Maintain contact persons.
- **Profile** - Edit company profile.
- **Settings** - Company settings and admins.

## Change association or company

You can use the organization switcher in the sidebar to switch between different clubs and companies if you have access to more than one.

## Header

In the header you will find:

- **Language switch** - Switch between German and English.
- **User menu** - Access your profile and log out.

## Footer

At the bottom of the sidebar you will find links to the imprint, privacy policy and the current version number.
