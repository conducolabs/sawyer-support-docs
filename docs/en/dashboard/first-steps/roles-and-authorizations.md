# Roles and authorizations

There are various roles in the dashboard that determine which areas and functions are available to you.

## Super Admin

As a super admin, you have access to all areas of the dashboard:

- All global administration areas (users, clubs, companies)
- All content areas (start page, categories, job types)
- Facilities and MünsterlandCard
- Communication (notifications, promotions)
- App settings, AI credits and activity log
- All club and company-specific areas

## Club admin

As a club admin you can:

- View the dashboard
- Manage events for assigned clubs
- View reporting
- Manage locations
- Edit club profiles and settings
- Manage company profiles and job offers

## Company admin

As a company admin you can:

- View the dashboard
- Manage your company's vacancies
- Maintain contact persons
- Edit company profile and settings
