## Overview

Logging in gives you access to all functions of the sawyer app. If you try to access the "News" or "Chats" tabs without being logged in, a login screen will automatically appear.

With your sawyer account, you can register for events, manage your profile, communicate with your club and access exclusive content. Registration is free and only takes a few minutes.

## Step-by-step

### Log in with an existing account

1. Open the sawyer app
2. Tap on the "News" or "Chats" tab - the login window will appear automatically
3. Enter your email address and password
4. Tap on "Sign in"

### Create a new account

1. Open the sawyer app
2. Tap on the "News" or "Chats" tab
3. Tap on "Register" or "Create new account" in the login window
4. Enter your personal data (name, e-mail, password)
5. Confirm your email address via the link you receive by email
6. Log in with your new access data

## FAQ

**I have forgotten my password. What can I do?
Tap on "Forgot password" on the login screen and enter your e-mail address. You will receive a link to reset your password.

**Can my child log in themselves?
No, children's accounts cannot register independently. You must create an account as a parent and can then add your child as a linked child account. This allows you to manage your child's logins and activities via your own account.

**Why can't I log in?
Make sure you are using the correct e-mail address and password. Make sure your password is case sensitive. If you have just registered, you must first confirm your e-mail address via the link in the confirmation e-mail.

**Do I have to create an account to use the app?
You can view a lot of content without an account. An account is required to register for events, chats and personalized content.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The confirmation email is not arriving.
**Solution:** Check your spam folder. If the e-mail is not there either, repeat the registration process or contact your club.

**Problem:** "Invalid login data" despite correct entry.
**Solution:** Reset your password via "Forgot password". If the problem persists, contact your club.
